ONLINE COURSES

WIRE THE WORLD (WIRING THE HOUSEHOLD APPLIANCES)

━━━

Course outline

Fundamentals of electricity and how it functions within the home appliances, thereby the students can take small electrical projects and repairs in their home, safely and competently.

Course Instructor

V. Vengatesan / Assistant Professor, Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering Prof. N. Subramanian / Assistant Professor, Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering Prof. G. Ram Prakash / Assistant Professor, Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Modules

Module 1 :                          Fundamentals of Electrical Knowledge and Practical Troubleshooting

Module 2 :                          Electrical Safety

Module 3 :                          Fitting and Rewinding of Celling Fan

Module 4 :                          Rectifier (How to Convert AC Supply into DC Supply)
