DEGREE

Students are provided with employability skills that will not only equip them with the knowledge, attitude and abilities for a future career

POST GRADUATION

The Placement Officer visits the 11 industrial hubs frequently , to discuss with HRs of various reputed companies. Subsequent to it, the HR team will have the glance of Infrastructural facilities and interact with the final year students. HRs will deliver the pre placement talks which comprises the Profile of the company, Selection process etc., then on a specified date , HR will shortlist the students after evaluating their performance in Aptitude test, Group discussion, Technical Interview and HR Interview.
